For this reason, your use of the mUzima mobile application is bound by the Google privacy policy with the use of Google services on the Android platform, that is available here: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/

      •  

    • b. The use of the mUzima mobile application is therefore bound by the Firebase privacy policy available here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

 

2. Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device’s internal memory.

mUzima app does not use these “cookies” explicitly. However, the app may use third party code and libraries that use “cookies” to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse the cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of this Service.

3. Location Data

mUzima mobile application comes with optional features that may need access to the precise or approximate location of the device. The features are:

    • a. Recording patient address: GPS addresses of patients may be stored as part of information stored in the secure OpenMRS medical record system. mUzima provides the ability to set the address of a patient record, so as to update the record in the OpenMRS medical record system.

      •  

    • b. Navigating to the patient’s address:
      mUzima provides the Geo Mapping feature to enable the user/provider to get directions (via Google maps app on Android device) to the patient’s GPS location as indicated by the medical record.

 

The GPS location information collected via the mobile application is purely for the features localized within the implementation and will in no way be shared with mUzima or any third party. The application may use the location information if you grant it the permissions to do so. You can enable or disable location services when you use our application at any time, through your device settings. Your implementation administrator can enable or disable the optional features that need location data via the application settings.
